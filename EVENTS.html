SINGLE TOURNAMENT
single tournament in tennis refers to a competition where one player competes against another,
with the loser of each match being eliminated until only one player remains as the champion.
This is also known as a single-elimination or knockout tournament.




DOUBLE TOURNAMENT
tennis double tournament refers to a competition format where teams of two players compete against each other,
These tournaments can be structured as single-elimination (where a team is eliminated after one loss)
or double-elimination (where two losses eliminate a team).
The term "double" in this context specifically means a doubles match,
not a double-elimination.




MIXED TOURNAMENT
Mixed tennis, also known as mixed doubles, is a tennis match where two teams of two players compete,
with each team consisting of one male and one female player.
It's a standard form of doubles tennis with the added element of mixed gender teams.
